## Slide 2: The Problem

### Clubs Are Leaking Revenue. Customers Are Overpaying.

**For Clubs:**
- Margins: 4–9% on alcohol
- CAC: ₹2,000–₹5,000 per head
- Retention: ~32% return rate
- Premium inventory sits dead on shelves
- Midweek demand collapses

**For Customers:**
- Pegs cost 20% more than a bottle — but who finishes a whole bottle in one night?
- No trust in physical bottle storage at clubs

> Customers *want* bottles (better value) but *can't* finish them. Clubs *want* repeat customers but have *no mechanism* to guarantee returns.

## Slide 3: The Solution

### Reserve a Bottle. Drink Across Visits.

1. Customer reserves bottle on MyBottle (e.g., "5 servings of JW Black Label")
2. Pays upfront via app — ~20% cheaper than 5 individual pegs
3. Visits club → generates single-use QR code
4. Bartender scans → validates → pours (10 seconds)
5. Comes back next week for remaining servings

> **1 bottle = 5 visits. Not 1.**

- Brand-level entitlement (not a physical bottle)
- Operational flow: 3 steps vs 6 (less friction than a normal peg order)
- Zero new hardware needed

**Visual:** Animated flow: Phone → QR → Scan → Pour → "4 servings remaining" notification.

## Slide 6: Business Model

### We Take 5–10% of Every Bottle Reserved

| Stream | Stage | Details |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Transaction fee (5–10%) | Day 1 | On every reservation |
| SaaS subscription | Phase 2 | Premium dashboard features |
| Featured placement | Phase 2 | Club visibility in customer app |
| Premium memberships | Phase 3 | Customer exclusive access |

**Unit Economics (₹5,000 bottle):**

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Customer saving vs pegs | ~20% (₹1,250) |
| MyBottle fee (8%) | ₹400 |
| Club margin | ₹1,600 (35%) |
| Ancillary revenue (4 return visits) | ~₹4,000 |
| **Total club value per reservation** | **₹5,600** |
| MyBottle net per txn | ₹300 |

> Everyone wins. Customer saves 20%. Club gets 5x visits. Breakage = zero-loss model for clubs.

# Appendix

## A1: Regulatory Positioning
- Technology platform — not alcohol seller/distributor/marketplace
- Alcohol owned and served by licensed venue
- Platform fee: IT services (SAC 998314, 18% GST)
- CA consultation flagged as pre-launch action item

## A2: Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Regulatory | Tech platform positioning; legal review |
| Club adoption | Free 3-month pilots; existing network |
| Customer non-return | 90-day expiry; breakage = club profit |
| Payment trust | Razorpay/Cashfree; T+1/T+2 settlement |
| Club closure | ToS clarity; vetting; holdback reserve |

## A3: Exit Strategy
1. Acquisition by hospitality/fintech (Zomato, Swiggy, Paytm)
2. Acquisition by global nightlife platform (Discotech, SevenRooms)
3. Standalone growth → IPO
- Likely: 3–5 years, 8–15x revenue
