# MyBottle -- Full Context Document

## Overview

MyBottle is a digital bottle wallet and hospitality infrastructure
platform for clubs and bars. It enables customers to reserve a full
liquor bottle as a prepaid serving entitlement, consume partially, and
store the remaining balance digitally for future visits.

## Core Concept

-   Bottle ownership is digitized as brand-level entitlement
    (not a specific physical bottle — any bottle of that brand in stock)
-   Each reservation can be consumed across multiple visits
-   Typically structured with a 5-serving model
-   QR-based redemption system
-   Managed inventory remains with the club

## Value Proposition

### For Customers

-   No wastage of bottles
-   Secure ownership tracking
-   Flexibility to consume over time
-   Cross-club redemption (if enabled)
-   Premium experience
-   Price advantage: bottle at club rate is ~20% cheaper than total
    equivalent peg cost across visits
-   Peg buyers convert to bottle reservers by spreading cost over visits

### For Clubs

-   Upfront full bottle payments
-   Increased average ticket size
-   Guaranteed repeat visits (retention engine)
-   Reduced inventory risk
-   Improved trust and transparency
-   Higher ancillary revenue (food, mixers, tables)
-   Breakage revenue: unredeemed servings = club keeps full payment
    AND retains remaining stock as credit (zero-loss model)

## Key Innovation: 5-Serving Model

-   Converts one bottle into 5 visits
-   Drives guaranteed repeat traffic
-   Improves LTV significantly
-   Creates predictable revenue

## Serving Validity Policy

-   Validity: 90 days from reservation
-   Expiry notifications at day 76
-   Grace extension: 30 days (club-configurable)
-   No refunds on expired servings
-   Expired servings = breakage revenue for club

## Business Model

-   Transaction fee: 5--10%
-   Subscription for clubs
-   Featured placement
-   Premium user memberships
-   SOR (Sale or Return) facilitation

## Payment Flow

-   Pass-through: customer pays via MyBottle app
-   MyBottle collects via payment aggregator (Razorpay/Cashfree)
-   Platform fee (5–10%) deducted, remainder settled to club (T+1/T+2)
-   Full transaction data captured

Traditional: - 1 visit per bottle - Uncertain repeat

MyBottle: - 5 visits per bottle - Guaranteed repeat - Higher lifetime
value - Breakage upside: unredeemed servings = full payment retained
+ stock credited back to club inventory
