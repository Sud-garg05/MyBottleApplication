## 3. The Problem

### Customer Side
- Pegs cost 20% more than a bottle — but who finishes a whole bottle in one night?
- No trust in physical bottle storage at clubs
- No digital identity for nightlife — your nights disappear
- Nightlife community is only physical — WhatsApp groups die by Monday
- No verified space to discover clubs, share opinions, find your people

### Club Side
- Margins: 4–9% on alcohol
- CAC: ₹2,000–₹5,000 per customer
- Retention: ~32% return rate
- Premium inventory sits dead on shelves
- Midweek demand collapses 60–70%
- No mechanism to guarantee repeat visits

---

## 4. The Solution

Mehfil is three layers stacked:

```
Layer 3: COMMUNITY    → India's first verified nightlife social layer
Layer 2: IDENTITY     → Your nightlife passport and status
Layer 1: WALLET       → Your bottle, your servings, your club
```

### Layer 1: The Wallet (Core Product)
- Reserve a bottle as a brand-level serving entitlement (e.g. "5 servings of JW Black Label")
- Pay upfront via app — ~20% cheaper than 5 individual pegs
- Visit club → generate premium full-screen QR code
- Bartender scans → validates → pours (10 seconds, 3 steps)
- Return for remaining servings — repeat visits guaranteed

### Layer 2: The Identity (Switching Cost)
- **Nightlife Passport:** Every visit, every bottle, every club — builds your permanent nightlife identity
- **Taste DNA:** Auto-derived from redemptions ("Single Malt / Light Cocktails")
- **Pour Score:** Behavioral trust score — unlocks premium access, priority Drops, tier perks
- **Annual Wrapped:** "Your 2026 Nightlife — 23 visits, 6 bottles, ₹18,000 saved"

### Layer 3: The Community (Moat)
- **Verified Reviews:** Only users with a redemption at that venue can review. No fake reviews.
- **Night Boards:** "Who's going to Vapour this Saturday?" — real-time coordination
- **Tribes:** Auto-generated interest communities ("Single Malt Crew Bangalore")
- **Legends:** City-level leaderboard — most visits, most generous, longest streak
- **The Drop:** Weekly limited releases — exclusive bottles, scarcity, members-only access

## 16. Regulatory Positioning

**Mehfil does NOT sell alcohol.**

- Technology platform + digital wallet + community
- Alcohol owned and served by licensed venue
- Platform fee: IT services (SAC 998314, 18% GST)
- No inventory ownership
- Age verification: DOB + Aadhaar/PAN via DigiLocker at registration
